Rationale

It all started with a bag of chips. Filled with so much air, uncontrollably loud, and contributing to the landfill. We wanted our company to make a positive impact on the environment and our users every day life. We designed a product that would be recyclable and would make an impact with every purchase.

For the commercial, we wanted to make a fun and memorable impact with our 15 seconds by highlighting one of our products unique feature. The goal was to capture the uncomfortable sensation of opening a bag of chips in a quiet setting and how our product offers a quieter solution.
